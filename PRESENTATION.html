Multi-Dimension Ranking Engine

Analyzes 8 signals across career, skills, JD fit, behavior, and activity to rank 100K candidates in 60-90 seconds.

8 Signals Driving Relevance

  • 📈 Career Progression (20%) — Role growth, seniority climb, lateral moves
  • 🎯 Skills Match (22%) — Semantic + exact skill alignment to JD
  • 📄 JD Fit (15%) — Deep semantic understanding of job requirements
  • 🏆 Title Tier (15%) — Seniority alignment & tier gating
  • ⏳ Experience (10%) — Years in role, depth, breadth
  • 🤝 Behavioral (10%) — Response rates, activity patterns
  • 📍 Location (4%) — Geography relevance, mobility
  • 🐙 GitHub Signal (4%) — Developer activity, contribution frequency

Total: 1.0 (100%)

Pipeline: Stage by Stage

Stage 1 | Honeypot & Timeline Detection
Filter out fake profiles & resume paradoxes (graduation dates, conflicting timelines)

Stage 2 | Tier Gating
Suppress Tier C (junior) candidates if role demands Tier A/B (mid/senior)

Stage 3 | Weighted Scoring
Apply 8 signals with configurable weights; compute 0-1 normalized scores

Stage 4 | Multipliers & Penalties
Boost strong matches, penalize weak fit; finalize top 100 shortlist
