Senior Machine Learning Engineer - Search & Recommendation Systems

We are looking for a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our team building 
state-of-the-art search and recommendation systems. You will work on dense retrieval,
semantic search, and learning-to-rank algorithms that power our core product.

Requirements:
- 5-8 years of experience in machine learning or related field
- Strong Python programming skills
- Experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow
- Knowledge of vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, Milvus)
- Familiarity with sentence-transformers and text embeddings
- Understanding of BM25 and information retrieval metrics (NDCG, MRR, MAP)
- Experience with hybrid search architectures (dense + sparse retrieval)
- Knowledge of learning-to-rank algorithms (LambdaMART, XGBoost ranking)

Preferred:
- PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field
- Experience with large-scale recommendation systems
- Knowledge of re-ranking and cross-encoder models
- Experience with Elasticsearch or OpenSearch
- Familiarity with FAISS for approximate nearest neighbor search
- Understanding of A/B testing frameworks for ranking systems

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement semantic search pipelines using dense embeddings
- Build and optimize recommendation algorithms for personalization
- Develop learning-to-rank models to improve search relevance
- Conduct A/B experiments to validate ranking improvements
- Collaborate with engineering teams to deploy models to production
- Monitor and optimize system performance (latency, throughput, accuracy)

Location: Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, or remote within India
Notice Period: 30 days or less preferred
